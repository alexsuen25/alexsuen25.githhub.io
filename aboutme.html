About Me

About me

I am a junior at Dublin High School for the 2022-2023 school year. Throughout my entire life, I have been passionate about computer science and programming. It is interesting and requires skills that I have. Also, it can benefit others through the technology programming can create and develop. I have taken and am going to take classes at Dublin High that revolve around computer science including Computer Science Essentials, AP Computer Science Principles, AP Computer Science Applications. In addition, I plan to go to a college that is focused on engineering and technology.

Throughout my experience with computer science, I have realized that I have some of the skills necessary to be successful in this field. I am organized, determined, and efficient. I have learned to be organized from my parents and also my teachers, who have all stressed out the importance of organizational skills. They help me focus and which in turn makes me feel better about my work. I am determined whenever I am passionate about the activity at hand. I am passionate about computer science and that translates to me being determined. I am also very efficient and love to improve my program’s efficiency. I also like to schedule my daily work and activities in an efficient manner. 

The experiences that I have had, the people that I have met, and the classes that I have taken all made me become a complete and passionate individual. They have shaped the characteristics that I have and my passions. Not only have they become role models for me, but they also soon realize that hard work does lead to success. 

My passion lies deep within my heart and nothing will ever change that. Computer science will remain my interest for the rest of my life and I will continue to advance myself to pursue higher goals every day. Thank you for your time and consideration in reading this letter and learning more about me. I hope you found it enlightening.
